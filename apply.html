Application Support FAQs

Why can't I apply directly online?

We provide personalized support to ensure your application is complete and accurate. Our specialists help prevent errors that could delay funding.

How long until I hear back?

You'll receive an initial response within 24 hours. A specialist will be assigned to guide you through the entire process.

Is Facebook secure for documents?

We use Facebook's encrypted messaging. For sensitive documents, we recommend email or phone consultation.

What if I don't have Facebook?

No problem! You can email us or call. All methods work equally well.

Can I apply for multiple programs?

Yes, discuss with your specialist which programs fit your situation best.

Is there any payment or fee?

No. Our support and application assistance costs you nothing. We do not charge for our help. If a grant program has its own required filing fees, those details will be in the application instructions. We are here to help you navigate that, but our services remain free.
