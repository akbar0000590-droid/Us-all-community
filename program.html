Our Grant Programs

Flexible funding opportunities designed for our community's diverse needs

Family Support Grant

$1,000 to $20,000

📋 Overview

Direct financial assistance for households with children, seniors, or dependents with special needs.

✅ Eligibility

  • Household income below 80% AMI (area median income)
  • At least one dependent child or senior (65+)
  • Reside in service area for 6+ months

📌 Supports

  • Rent/mortgage assistance
  • Utility bills and groceries
  • Childcare or after-school programs
Next deadline: Rolling applications (reviewed monthly)

Micro-Enterprise Grant

$20,000 to $100,000

📋 Overview

Startup or growth capital for very small businesses, side hustles, and freelance entrepreneurs.

✅ Eligibility

  • Business with 1-5 employees (including owner)
  • In operation 6+ months or pre-launch with solid plan
  • Annual revenue under $100,000

📌 Supports

  • Equipment and supplies
  • Marketing and website development
  • Workspace or inventory
Next deadline: Quarterly
Check eligibility →

Creative Entrepreneur Grant

$100,000 to $650,000

📋 Overview

Support for artists, makers, musicians, and cultural workers to create and sustain their practice.

✅ Eligibility

  • Active creative practice for 1+ year
  • Reside in one of the 12 partner counties
  • Demonstrated community engagement

📌 Supports

  • Materials and studio space
  • Exhibition or performance costs
  • Professional development
Next deadline: Annually

Program FAQs

Can I apply for more than one grant?

Yes, but only one award per 12-month period. You must submit separate applications.

What if I don't have all documents?

Our navigators can help you obtain birth certificates, tax records, or business licenses at no cost.

Are these grants taxable?

Generally, grants are considered taxable income. We provide a 1099 form if award exceeds $600.

How are funds distributed?

Direct deposit or prepaid debit card within 5 business days after approval.
