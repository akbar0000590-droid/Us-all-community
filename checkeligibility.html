Program Requirements by Category

Family Support

  • Income below 80% Area Median Income
  • At least one dependent (under 18 or 65+)
  • Valid government ID
  • Proof of residency
  • Social Security Number or ITIN
💼

Micro-Enterprise

  • 1-5 employees (including owner)
  • Annual revenue under $100,000
  • In operation 6+ months or detailed plan
  • Business license or EIN
  • No active bankruptcy
🎨

Creative Entrepreneur

  • Active creative practice for 1+ year
  • Portfolio or work samples
  • Reside in any US state or territory
  • Demonstrated community involvement
  • Not a full-time student

Frequently Asked Questions

I live in a US territory (Puerto Rico, Guam, etc.). Can I apply?

Yes! We accept applications from all US states and territories. Income guidelines are adjusted for cost of living.

What if my county isn't listed?

All 3,243 US counties and county-equivalents are in our database. If you can't find yours, please contact us.

Do I need to be a US citizen?

No. We accept ITIN, foreign passports, and consular IDs. Your immigration status does not affect eligibility.

How are income limits determined?

We use HUD's 80% Area Median Income (AMI) guidelines, updated annually for every county.

Can I apply from any state?

Yes, we serve applicants in all 50 states. Some programs have geographic priorities, but everyone is considered.
